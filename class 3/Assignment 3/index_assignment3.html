Joss Bites

Joss Bites was established in 2018 by legendary restauranteur Cecile Tang of Joss Cuisine, Beverly Hills.
She saw the future of food and knew that she wanted to create a fast casual establishment with a high tech ordering style while keeping traditional flavors in her dishes.
At Joss Bites, you will review a repertoire of bites curated throughout Asia. Enjoy!
